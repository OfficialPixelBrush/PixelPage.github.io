7-Segment Decoder ROM

This idea originally came from me wanting to build a fully digital clock from TTL-Chips and Diodes. Here's the Logisim circuit for that. To not need 4 separate decoding ROMs, I figured just using 4 Registers would work instead. Later I realized just flashing the 4 numbers quickly would work too.
