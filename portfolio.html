Intro


Hello! I'm Pixel Brush!

I've been practicing animation and art in general since the Summer of 2016.
I'm best known for being a reliable friend and working fast but rough, also for #9C6868 backgrounds.

My programming career dates back further, to about 2010, where I spent most of my primary school days editing the template programs that came with GamerMaker Lite, the earliest surviving projects dating back to 2015.

My latest ventures have brought me to making some decent looking paintings, some live action sketches with VFX and Voice Acting in various things.

I'm also looking into approaching some larger animation projects, but those need plenty of time and maybe even a small crew behind them.
